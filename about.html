Mason Mullanax

Welcome everyone! My name is Mason Mullanax. Just like countless others I fell in love with gmaes at a young age. I got my first console, the Nintendo 64, when I was 4 years old and Ocarina of Time soon became all I cared about. I recall having binders with hundreds of pages of walkthroughs, and when bosses got too scary, I'd have my mom come in and beat it for me, while I went to go hide in another room. Games have come a long way since then and so have I We are fortunate to live in a time where video games have become mainstream and studios keep pumping out games. Of course, the downside of more games is that not all of them are good. My goal is to become another voice in a herd of game reviewers, but one with a consistent voice, so the reader can get an idea of what games I like and don't like. The problem I see with major games publications is that they lose their consistency with the amount of journalists they have working. Some journalists will rave about a game, while others despise it all while speaking for the same brand. In my experience, its better to have a good understanding of solo reviewers work becuase you understand where they are coming from with game elements they like and dislike. So here I am, willing and ready to give my voice.

About this website

This website is 100% ad-free and does not accept, nor will ever accept, free copies of games. Pricing absolutely matters when reviewing a game, just as with any other product. A consumer needs to feel like they've got got their money's worth. The objective of this website is to give personal opinions and reviews of games and the games industry. Therefore, not everything will be objective or expressed in a way everyone will agree with.
